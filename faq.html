Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I get a refund after purchasing a product?

Refund is only possible if there is an issue from our side (such as failure in delivery by Team Arpita Mehta). The refund process will take place within 30 working days.

What is the return policy?

Cancellations are not possible once an order is placed. If you wish to return a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our customer care at info@arpitamehtaofficial.com or call us on +91 22 000000 within 1 day of receipt. You can return the product through Speed Post and provide tracking details.

What should I do if the product I received is damaged?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, kindly send a snapshot of the outer packaging and the product to our customer care. You will have 1 day after receipt to contact us for a return. The returned item will be reviewed and the correct product will be sent back within 7 working days.

When will I not be eligible for a refund?

  • If the shipping address provided is incorrect.
  • If the product has been partially used or damaged after receipt.
  • If the product is already in transit.
  • If our return policy guidelines are not followed.

Can I get an exchange instead of a refund?

Yes, exchange is possible if:

  • The delivered products are damaged or opened.
  • The company delivers the wrong products by mistake.

We will process the exchange within 7 working days after receiving the product.

How long does it take to receive a refund for a duplicate transaction?

If you accidentally paid twice for the same transaction, the duplicate amount will be refunded within 20 working days via the same source of payment.

Is it possible to cancel my order?

Once an order is placed, cancellation is not allowed. If you attempt to cancel after dispatch, the cancellation will not be accepted.

What should I do if I received an incomplete order?

If any units are missing from your order, please reach out to us at info@arpitamehtaofficial.com within 1 day of receipt. Include a snapshot of the outer packaging and the products received.
